About

For almost 26 years now, we have been creating beautiful and unique Alberta timber frames. Our past projects have included private residences, commercial buildings, Polo Stables, a Church and even a Best Western Hotel.

Our timber frames have been shipped throughout Canada, the US Pacific Northwest, Montana, and even Japan.

Design

We can design and build any timber frame, to any budget, for any home you have in mind. We do not offer a 'plan book'. A timber frame house is as original as the homeowner, and we would like to custom design your home to fit your current and future plans.

There are not many construction constraints on framing a timber frame home like on a conventionally framed home, so you can be creative and do almost anything you want!

It can be very hard to visualize your timber frame home but with our 3-D isometric drawings and video fly-throughs you can see exactly what it will look like from various different views.
